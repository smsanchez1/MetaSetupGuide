### **Cast to a TV using Google Chromecast**

**Requirements**

* A **Google Chromecast** device (or a smart TV with Chromecast built-in).
* The **Google Home** app installed on your phone.
* All devices on the **same Wi-Fi network**.

#### **Cast from the Meta Quest headset**

1. Put on your **Meta Quest 2 or 3** headset.
2. Press the **Meta (Oculus)** button on the right controller to open the **Universal Menu**.
3. Select the **clock** on the left-hand side to open **Quick Settings**.
4. Tap **Cast**.
5. In the list of devices, select your **Chromecast or TV**.
6. Select **Next**, and the TV will begin showing the headset display.

**To stop casting**

* Open **Quick Settings → Cast → Stop Casting.**
