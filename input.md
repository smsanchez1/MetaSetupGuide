## **Connecting with Meta Quest Link**

Meta Quest Link allows you to connect your headset to a Windows PC to play PC VR content, use Remote Desktop, or share your screen.

### **Requirements**

* **Windows 10 or 11** (64-bit)
* **8 GB RAM** minimum (16 GB recommended)
* **USB 3.0 port** or **Wi-Fi 5 GHz** for Air Link
* Compatible **GPU** (NVIDIA GTX 970 / AMD 400 series or newer)
* At least **4 GB of free disk space**

---

### **Install the Meta Quest Link App**

1. On your computer, go to **[www.meta.com/quest/setup](https://www.meta.com/quest/setup)**.
2. Download and install the **Meta Quest Link app**.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete setup.

![ meta quest link app ](https://github.com/smsanchez1/MetaSetupGuide/blob/main/srcs/img/meta-quest-link-app.jpg?raw=true)

---

### **Connect Your Headset**

#### **Option A – Link Cable**

1. Plug a **USB-C 3.2 cable** into your computer and the headset.
2. Put on the headset and open the **Quick Settings** menu.
3. Select **Link → Computer → Launch** to start PC VR mode.

#### **Option B – Air Link (Wireless)**

1. Ensure your **PC and headset** are on the **same 5 GHz Wi-Fi** network.
2. On your headset:

   * Press the **Meta (Oculus)** button → open **Quick Settings** → select **Link**.
   * Enable **Use Air Link** and choose your PC.
3. Confirm the pairing code on both devices.
4. Select **Launch** to start using Air Link.

---

### **Disable or Manage Link**

* Open **Quick Settings → Settings → Link**.
* Use the toggle to enable or disable **Link** or **Air Link** as needed.
